About Sci-Flix

Welcome to Sci-Flix, your ultimate database of science fiction films. We're passionate about sci-fi cinema and have created this platform to provide comprehensive information about the genre's most influential and interesting works.

Our Mission

Sci-Flix aims to catalog and preserve information about science fiction cinema, making it accessible to fans, researchers, and film enthusiasts worldwide. We believe that science fiction, as a genre, offers unique perspectives on technology, society, and humanity's future, and deserves dedicated study and appreciation.

Features

  • Comprehensive Database: Access information about hundreds of sci-fi films, from early classics to modern masterpieces.
  • Detailed Information: Each film entry includes plot summaries, director information, genre classifications, and more.
  • User Accounts: Create an account to save your favorite films and customize your experience.
  • API Access: Developers can access our database programmatically through our well-documented API.

Technology

Sci-Flix is built using modern web technologies, with a Node.js backend, MongoDB database, and responsive frontend design. Our API is RESTful and provides JSON responses for easy integration with other applications.
